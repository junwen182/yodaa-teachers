GET STARTED

How Yodaa works for Teachers

STEP 1

We send you customer requests.

Customers visit yodaa to tell us their teaching needs. We verify such requests to ensure they’re genuine, and send them to you.

STEP 2

Decide if you want the job.

When a request is a good fit, send a quote and introduce yourself. We automatically send your business profile containing your past experience and contact details to the customer. Each request can only receive up to 5 quotes.

STEP 3

Get hired.

The customer reviews the quotes and decides whom to contact and hire. If you’re hired, make a good impression and secure a review from the customer. Good reviews help greatly in getting future jobs.

How to get hired

BUILD A WINNING PROFILE
  • Promote your teaching experience and skills.
  • Talk about your qualifications, certifications, licenses etc.
  • Include photographs of yourself and your students.
Teach well and ask for reviews

When you’ve been an inspiration, ask for reviews from customers. This helps greatly in getting future jobs.

MAKE A PERSONAL CONNECTION
  • Be clear & professional in your intro to the customer.
  • Give a detailed and competitive quote.
  • If you can’t quote, give an estimate or price range.
FOLLOW UP
  • Follow up with the customer after sending a quote.
  • Keep trying. Persistence is key.
  • Ask for help if needed. We’re happy to assist.
